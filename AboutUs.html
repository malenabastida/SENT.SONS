About Us

The mission of SentSons is to advance public awareness of the benefits of music therapy and increase access to quality music therapy services in a rapidly changing world.

Our wonderful team assesses emotional well-being, physical health, social functioning, communication abilities, & cognitive skills through musical responses; designs music interventions for individuals and groups based on client needs; & follows up marvelously on suscriptors' progress.
